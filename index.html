Hack2TechSustain: Where Innovation Meets Sustainability.

Welcome to Hack2TechSustain, an initiative organized by the Department of Computer Technology at MIT Campus, Anna University, in collaboration with Auriseg Cyber Security. At Hack2TechSustain, we are committed to fostering innovation and sustainability through the power of technology. Join Us in Shaping a Sustainable Future Through Technology Innovation!

Together, let's harness the power of innovation to build a sustainable future for generations to come. Join us and be a catalyst for change at Hack2TechSustain!

MIT, Chennai, India

HACKATHON THEME: SUSTAINABILITY THROUGH INNOVATION

Hack2TechSustain harnesses technology to tackle global sustainability challenges, offering themes like cybersecurity, natural language processing, computer vision, and Industry 4.0 for participants to develop innovative solutions promoting environmental, social, and economic sustainability.
